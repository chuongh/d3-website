No-shows cost your salon money and leave technicians sitting idle during prime hours. A few small process changes can dramatically cut how often clients miss their appointments. Here are seven approaches that work.

## Send Automatic Reminders

Text and email reminders before the appointment give clients a chance to confirm or reschedule. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}

## Require Deposits for High-Demand Slots

Asking for a deposit on long or peak-time services reduces casual no-shows. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}

## Make Booking and Rescheduling Easy

When clients can rebook in seconds, they are less likely to simply not show. A smooth [online booking](/online-booking) flow makes a big difference. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}
