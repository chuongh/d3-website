Turn-based payroll is the backbone of how most nail salons pay their technicians. It rotates clients fairly and ties each technician's pay to the tickets they actually serve. Here is how it works and how to stop tracking it by hand.

## What Turn-Based Payroll Means

In a turn system, technicians take clients in rotation, and pay is calculated from the tickets and tips tied to each turn. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}

## Why Manual Turn Tracking Breaks Down

Tracking turns on paper or a whiteboard leads to disputes, missed tips, and slow end-of-day math. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}

## Automating Turns and Payroll

A connected [payroll](/payroll) system records every turn at the front desk and calculates pay automatically. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}
