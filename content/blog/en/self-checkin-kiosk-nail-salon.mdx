A self check-in kiosk lets clients sign in on an iPad the moment they walk in, without waiting for the front desk. For busy nail salons, that can mean shorter lines and happier clients. But is it right for your shop?

## How a Self Check-In Kiosk Works

Clients tap in on a kiosk or scan a QR code, and the system adds them to the queue automatically. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}

## The Benefits for Busy Salons

A [check-in](/check-in) kiosk frees your front desk during rush hours and captures client info for loyalty programs. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}

## When a Kiosk Might Not Be Worth It

Smaller salons with light walk-in traffic may not see the same payoff, so weigh your volume first. {/* TODO: real copy needed */}
